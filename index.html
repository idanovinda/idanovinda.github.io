I am master student of computer science in Saarland University (2018-now). I received my bachelor degree of computer science in Universitas Gadjah Mada (2014-2018). My bachelor thesis was doing classification on description question’s answer for Indonesian national exam using convolutional neural network and GloVe as the word embedding. The project was associated with the Indonesian Ministry of Education and Culture.

Before joining Saarland University, I have some industry experiences. I was working as a Data Engineer for 6 months in DALA Technology (2017-2018). During my work in DALA Technology, I was working on a project called Pusdatin (Integrated Data System). This project was associated with Indonesian Ministry of Energy and Mineral Resources and Pertamina. I was also employeed as a Data Scientist in Kulina (2018). My works were mostly doing data analysis on customer data.

I am interested to do research on natural language processing, machine learning, and information retrieval. I write articles about machine learning in my medium in Bahasa Indonesia. Check it out!
